# Warden Summoner v1 by YuriSquid
## MINECRAFT DATAPACK
**Minecraft Version: 1.21+ / 26.2+ (Preferably 26.2+)**
**Description: Summons 5 allied Wardens as your personal bodyguards.**

## __HOW TO INSTALL__
1. Place the **Warden Summoner v1 by YuriSquid (26.2)** folder into your world's datapacks folder.
2. Open your world.
3. Type `/reload` in the game chat. You should see a green **"Warden Summoner Datapack Loaded!"** message.
3a. If `/reload` doesn't work, type `/function warden_pack:load`


## __HOW TO USE__
1. Ensure you have the **"Warden Heart"** (Custom Conduit) somewhere in your inventory, hotbar, or hands.
2. Drop a **"Warden Soul"** (Custom Nether Star) onto the ground (Press Q).
3. Wait exactly 2 seconds or 40 in-game ticks.
4. Lightning will strike, the Warden Soul will be consumed, and 5 allied Wardens will rise from the ground!
Note: These Wardens are automatically assigned to your team **("Warden Allies")** so they will ruthlessly attack hostile mobs but ignore you. They are also given a permanent dig cooldown memory so they stay above ground to defend you instead of despawning.


## __FUNCTION COMMANDS__
**Do NOT rename regular items in an anvil!** The datapack requires specific hidden custom_data components to work. Use these built-in commands in your chat to retrieve the exact required items:
- To get the Summoner Conduit ("Warden Heart"): `/function warden_pack:give_warden_heart`
- To get 1 Stack (64) of "Warden Souls" (Nether Stars): `/function warden_pack:give_warden_souls`
- To get a full Dark Green Shulker Box of Warden Souls (1,728 total): `/function warden_pack:give_soul_shulker`


## __TROUBLESHOOTING__
- Nothing happens? Make sure you used the commands above to get the items, not an anvil.
- Timer not starting? Make sure the Warden Soul has physically touched the ground.
- See a scoreboard on your screen? Type `/reload` and it will automatically hide itself.
